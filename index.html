ABOUT

When renovating their own apartments, the Möbelita founders realized how difficult it is to select the appropriate furniture. In the process, we discovered several italian producers who fascinated us with their quality and style, and we wanted to share our fascination with the world. Thus, Möbelita was born in 2020. From the start, we did not want to be just another furniture store. We created an app which allowed to configure our furniture and to display it in Augmented Reality (AR) in the customer‘s interior This app raised interest of the furniture suppliers. Thus, ConfiGuru software was born. Today, ConfiGuru could configure the complete apartments, as well as the furniture. And furthermore, it can configure and visualize cars, bicycles, and much more.

Diminished Reality

By utilizing computer vision algorithms and image processing techniques, diminished reality alters the perception of the physical world by eliminating specific content in real-time. Our technology aims to enhance user experiences, increase productivity, and provide seamless integration between the virtual and real worlds.
